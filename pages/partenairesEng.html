The Proximity Partnership

What is the Proximity Partnership?

By becoming a sponsor of one of our academicians, you actively and significantly contribute to the social development of our community. This proximity partnership offers the unique opportunity to establish a personal connection with a young person by tracking their progress and actively participating in their sustainable independence.

Who benefits from this program?

The Proximity Partnership aims to support children and young people aged 6 to 26. Whether it's children whose parents wish to offer them a quality activity, young adults looking to define or fulfill their socio-professional integration project, or isolated minor migrants seeking to integrate, every academician has their place here.

Why is this program important?

At Massy Academy, we firmly believe that a caring environment and adequate support can transform a child or young person's life. Through proximity sponsorship, we not only support our academicians' personal development but also their families, while promoting access to quality education. Our goal is to offer everyone an equal chance of success, regardless of their circumstances.

Who are our sponsors?

Our sponsors come from all walks of life: responsible companies wishing to invest meaningfully in the community, to committed and supportive individuals, eager to support a cause close to their hearts.

How to join our Partner Club?

By joining the Massy Academy Partner Club, you do more than support a young person; you assert your brand image and responsible employer identity. Whether you are a company or an individual, your donations to the Massy Academy are eligible for a tax reduction, allowing you to give while benefiting from tax advantages.

We believe in equal opportunities and with your support, we can help more young people achieve their dreams. Join us today and together, let's make a difference.
